Who are we and what do we do at Kent Learning

Kent Learn was born from the vision of Alvine Munene, the founder of Kent Ventures a vision rooted in the belief that technology should be for everyone, not just a select few.

What started as Kent Ventures’ mission to grow the tech space has evolved into a learning movement that empowers people to understand, use, and thrive with modern technology. Every day, the world changes a little faster, and new innovations emerge at lightning speed.

At Kent Learn, we make sure no one is left behind — giving learners the skills, confidence, and opportunities they need to turn technology into a tool for growth, creativity, and success
